🗺️ District Price Increase (2024–2025)

Explore how average housing prices changed across Seoul's 25 districts with interactive spatial analysis.

📍 Key Insights — 2024–2025 Map

  • River Corridor Momentum: Notable increases are concentrated around the Han River—especially Gangnam, Songpa, Mapo, and Seongdong.
  • Regional Contrast: Districts in the southwest and northeast show relatively stable price points compared to the central business hubs.
  • Premium Persistence: Traditional high-value districts continue to lead the recovery in market sentiment.
