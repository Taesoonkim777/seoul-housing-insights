📈 District Growth

Housing price growth rates and patterns across Seoul's 25 districts.

The visualizations below explore how housing prices have grown across Seoul's districts over time. From interactive drill-down maps to animated temporal views, each chart reveals different dimensions of district-level price dynamics.

1. Drilldown Map

Interactive map of Seoul showing real estate price changes by district. Click on a district to drill down into sub-regions for detailed price analysis.

2. Line Chart — Price per Area

District-level average price per area over time. Compare how different districts have experienced varying rates of price appreciation across the timeline.

3. Heatmap — Increase of Price per Area

Heatmap visualization highlighting the magnitude of price increases per area across Seoul's districts, revealing spatial patterns of growth intensity.

4. Animated Map — Price Increase

Animated choropleth map showing the progression of price increases over time across Seoul's districts, illustrating how growth patterns evolved year by year.
