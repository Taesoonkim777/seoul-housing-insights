💡 Recommendations

Policy Recommendation

Data-driven policy directions to address Seoul's housing price inequality and improve urban livability.

🚇

Expand Transit Infrastructure

Extend subway lines and improve bus networks in under-served northern districts to reduce the amenity gap that drives price inequality between Gangbuk and Gangnam.

⚖️

Regulation

Impact and effectiveness of real estate regulation policies.

View details
🏫

Equalize Education Quality

Invest in public school quality across all districts. The concentration of top-ranked schools in Gangnam is a primary driver of housing demand and price premiums.

🏗️

Targeted Supply Expansion

Increase housing supply in high-demand areas through urban renewal and rezoning, rather than blanket regulation that restricts market activity.

🌳

Green Space Development

Create new parks and green corridors in districts with low green space ratios to improve livability and attract balanced development.

📋

Smart Regulation Design

Shift from demand-suppression policies (loan restrictions, tax hikes) to supply-side and amenity-based approaches that address root causes of price disparity.

📈

Data-Driven Monitoring

Establish a real-time urban amenity index to monitor district-level livability and guide public investment decisions for equitable urban development.
