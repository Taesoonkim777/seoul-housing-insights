🗺️ 2024–2025 District Price Increase

Explore how average housing prices changed from 2024 to 2025 across Seoul’s 25 districts.

📈 Key Insights — 2024–2025 Increase

  • Notable increases are concentrated around the Han River corridor.
  • Gangnam-gu, Songpa-gu, and Seongdong-gu show the strongest upward momentum.
  • Price appreciation in riverside areas significantly outpaces the city average.

📊 Key Insights — Price per Area Trend

  • Comparison of district-level appreciation rates over time.
  • Identifies which districts are leading the recovery in the housing market.
  • Shows a widening gap between high-demand areas and the rest of Seoul.

🔥 Key Insights — Growth Intensity

  • Visualizes the intensity of price changes per unit area.
  • Highlights specific "hotspots" where demand is rapidly increasing.
  • Provides a clear contrast between stable districts and volatile growth areas.

✨ Key Insights — Evolution of Growth

  • Animated view showing how growth patterns shifted throughout the year.
  • Observes the ripple effect of price increases starting from the core districts.
  • Useful for understanding the temporal flow of the Seoul real estate market.
