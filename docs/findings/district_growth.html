🗺️ 2024–2025 District Price Increase

Explore how average housing prices changed from 2024 to 2025 across Seoul’s 25 districts, utilizing Seoul Real Estate Actual Transaction Price Open API data (서울시 실거래가 공시 API).

📈 Key Insights — 2024–2025 Increase Map

  • Riverside Concentration: Growth is heavily concentrated along the Han River corridor, particularly in Seongdong, Mapo, and Yongsan.
  • Gangnam Leadership: The southern cluster (Gangnam, Seocho, Songpa) continues to show the most resilient upward momentum.
  • Regional Disparity: A clear performance gap exists between high-demand business hubs and peripheral residential districts.
  • Urban Core Premium: Districts with proximity to major employment centers exhibit higher appreciation rates per square meter.
